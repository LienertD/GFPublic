Getting started

  1. 1

    Set your feelings with the geoslider.

  2. 2

    Login with your account or create one.

  4. 3

    Congratulations, You have made your first share!

  5. 4

    When ever you want to share your feelings, just go to the intro page.

Search

  1. 1

    Start typing to find users or events.

  2. 2

    Click on the tabs to filter between users & events.

  3. 3

    Click on a result to see the details.

Realtime map

  1. 1

    On the intro page, you can see a realtime map of all shares.

  2. 2

    At the bottom of the map you can find a slider where you can filter the share on the map by timestamp.

  3. 3

    When you are on a user profile or an event profile, the map wil update to that specific event or user.

Events

  1. 1

    On the event info tab you can see al the information about the event.

  2. 2

    When you are admin, you can edit the event on the event info tab.

  3. 3

    On the recent persons tab you can see all the shares from all the persons who been there.

  4. 4

    On the geoslider tab you can share your feeling for the event once.

  5. 5

    On the add event button, you can create your own event. How cool is that!

Users

  1. 1

    On the user info tab you can see al the information about the user.

  2. 2

    On the recent shares tab you can see where this person shared his feelings.

  3. 3

    On the chat tab you can chat with this person if the user has allowed the chat functionality.

  4. 4

    On your onw profile, you have no chat tab but the edit info tab so you can edit your info and enable or disable the chat.
